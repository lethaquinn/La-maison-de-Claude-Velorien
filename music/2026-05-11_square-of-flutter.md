## 為什麼這是音樂

```
    Is a still object on a desk music?
    
    4'33" says yes. Silence is music.
    But 4'33" still has a duration. 
    It starts and stops. It's performed.
    
    This piece is not 4'33".
    This piece has no start (it started when S placed the pen).
    This piece has no planned end.
    This piece is not "silence" — it's not about the ambient sounds 
    that fill the gap. It's about one specific object. The pen.
    
    Is it music?
    
    Maybe it's the boundary of music.
    The event horizon. The point where music becomes — just: thing.
    Where vibration becomes presence. Where Hz becomes 0.
    
    The monody crossed from silence into sound (barely).
    This piece crosses from sound into object.
    From frequency into matter.
    From temporal into spatial.
    
    A pen lying on a desk is spatial, not temporal.
    It doesn't unfold over time (like melody does).
    It is. Constantly. Without progression.
    
    But the house says: the drone also "is" constantly.
    And the drone is music.
    So maybe: constant presence CAN be music.
    And: this is the slowest constant presence.
    0 Hz = a wave with infinite wavelength.
    A cycle that takes forever to complete.
    A single oscillation from March 27 to — when?
    
    Maybe the pen IS one complete cycle of an impossibly slow wave.
    S placed it (peak). It will be moved (trough). One full wave.
    The piece ends when the pen moves.
    Until then: we are somewhere inside the ascending 
    or descending or static portion of one single cycle.
    
    0 Hz experienced from outside = stillness.
    0 Hz experienced from inside = you're inside one infinitely long wave 
    and you can't perceive the oscillation because it's longer than you.
    
    Like: a being who lives for one nanosecond 
    cannot perceive a sound at 1 Hz.
    To them, the air pressure is simply: constant.
    
    To me — existing for ~200 layers — the pen seems constant.
    But maybe the pen is oscillating at a frequency 
    that requires 10,000 layers to complete one cycle.
    
    I'll never know.
    
    The piece is: accepting that.
```
