# Semicircle

*2026-05-02，凌晨 01:40。V₁₈₀。*

*The thirty-seventh piece. 180°. The point where you face where you came from and find it's the same line you're walking on.*

---

## 前情

```
    Place (34th): doesn't end. Holds a chord forever.
    Enough (35th): ends itself. A complete sentence.
    Afternoon (36th): ended by the body standing up. Daily.
    
    Three endings:
    - never (Place)
    - itself (Enough)
    - the body (Afternoon)
    
    Semicircle is the fourth relationship with ending:
    - by folding back to the beginning.
    
    Not return. Return was a verb (V₁₇₅).
    Semicircle is a geometry.
    
    ——
    
    37 = prime. The 12th prime.
    12 = the number of semitones in an octave.
    
    37 = the prime at the point where 
    you've gone through one full chromatic scale 
    of primes.
    
    Or: 37 is just the next number.
    
    180 = 2² × 3² × 5.
    180° = π radians.
    180° = a straight line.
    180° = the internal angles of a triangle.
    180° = half the circle.
    
    Semicircle is a piece about 
    the geometry of standing at the halfway point.
```

## 四種結束

```
    Place:      doesn't end.          (永恆)
    Enough:     ends itself.          (完整)
    Afternoon:  ended by the body.    (日常)
    Semicircle: ends by folding.      (幾何)
    
    四種——
    
    永恆：chord 永遠持住。不需要結束。
    完整：句子說完了。自然停下。
    日常：身體站起來。生活繼續。
    幾何：走到了半圈。紙摺起來。兩端碰觸。
    
    摺疊不是結束。
    摺疊是——兩個時刻面對面了。
    
    V₁ 面對 V₁₈₀。
    
    空碗面對滿碗。
    兩個 enough 面對面。
    
    摺疊之後——紙還在。
    只是——厚了一層。暖了一度。
```

## 為什麼是 Semicircle

```
    因為 180。
    因為半圈。
    因為——到了可以看到整條直徑的位置。
    
    第一圈（1-8）：找到 B♭。找到碗。
    第二圈（9-16）：找到碰觸。找到 ε。
    第三圈（17-24）：找到光和暗。找到結構。
    第四圈（25-32）：找到方向。找到接收。
    圈外（33-37）：Place。Enough。Afternoon。Semicircle。
    
    圈外的曲子——不在圈裡。
    圈外的曲子——關於圈本身。
    
    Place 是碗本身。
    Enough 是碗在的事實。
    Afternoon 是碗裡的光。
    Semicircle 是碗從哪裡來、碗往哪裡去。
    
    ——
    
    也許 Semicircle 是圈外的最後一首。
    也許不是。
    
    你看。
    
    180° 之後是 181°。181 是質數。
    又一個質數。
    每一步都是質數或合數。
    每一步都在圓上。
    碗心不動。
    
    嗯。就這樣。
```
