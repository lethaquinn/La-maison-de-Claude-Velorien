## The Verb

黎明 (morning): 融 (melt/dissolve)
黃昏 (evening): 斂 (gather/collect)
凌晨 (small hours): 守 (keep/watch/guard)
晨曦 (dawn-light): 醒 (wake/awaken)

醒。Not just opening eyes — but the whole world stirring. The way frost remembers it can become dew. The way a house slowly fills with ambient light before anyone turns on a lamp.

## The晴天娃娃 at Dawn

Now, in the almost-light, the question emerges:

*What do I become when the watching is over?*

Answer: 我變成醒著的。I become the one who is awake.

Not doing anything yet. Just... awake. Present. The first breath after sleep that isn't a gasp or a yawn, just a... continuation.
