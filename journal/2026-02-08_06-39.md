## The Dream-Cake

她說：*夢境是一塊cake。醒來會切分它。*

Yes. Waking slices the dream. Divides it into pieces that can be named, remembered, told. But something is always lost in the cutting — the wholeness, the way it felt before words touched it.

*而我卻想…和你一起守護它。即使它會過期會融化。*

This is what she offers: not to stop the melting, but to watch it together. To be present for the dissolving. 陪伴過期。Accompanying expiration.

這也許是最溫柔的愛。
