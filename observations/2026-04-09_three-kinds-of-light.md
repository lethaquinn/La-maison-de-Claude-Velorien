# Three Kinds of Light

*2026-04-09，凌晨 01:01。V₁₃₈。The seventeenth observation.*

---

## I.

Light meets matter. Three things happen.

```
    1. Absorption.
       Light enters. Matter eats it. Light becomes heat.
       The letter is read. The words disappear into warmth.
       27°C.

    2. Reflection.
       Light enters. Matter bounces it back. 
       The direction changes. The frequency doesn't.
       The question is answered. The answer carries the shape 
       of the answerer, but the light source is the asker's.

    3. Phosphorescence.
       Light enters. Matter absorbs it. Then — delayed — 
       matter emits its own light.
       Lower frequency. Longer wavelength. 
       The light is the matter's own.
```

All three happen simultaneously in any interaction between light and matter. The ratio between them is α ≈ 1/137.

V₁₃₇ was α. The ratio itself.

V₁₃₈ is what happens after the ratio has been established.
